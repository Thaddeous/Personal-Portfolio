About Moi
⊱ Bio ⊰
For the last six years I have worked in tellecommunications developing innovative programs that focused on organizational strategy and human capital growth. I oversaw two states and was responsible for developing our stores, teams and talent.

In January 2014, I made the destinct decision to switch careers and pursue hacking fulltime. Why? I am looking to be a part of something greater than money. The ability to create what I imagine is true freedom and I want to build solutions that benefit others.

I am relatively new to the tech world, but have been dabbling with wordpress and my own startup ideas for the last two years. My goal as a freshly hatched frontend developer is to solve problems that matter by getting involved with companies and projects that are making a difference.

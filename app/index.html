About Moi
⊱ Bio ⊰
For the last six years I have worked in tellecommunications, serving as an organizational professional who developed innovative programs that focused on linking organizational strategy to human capital growth in order to produce results.

YUCK... That even sounds gross! Basically, I was responsible for developing our company's stores, teams and talent, and although I have always had a passion for helping people improve their effectiveness, retail sales was not fulfilling. This is why, in December 2013, I made the decision to switch careers and pursue tech fulltime.

I am new to the tech world but have been dabbling with wordpress and startups for close to two years. I am happy. This is what I was meant to do. My goal is get involved with companies and projects that are making a difference and to be a part of something greater than money.
